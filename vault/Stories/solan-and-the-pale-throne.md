# Solan and the Pale Throne

*A farmer's son ends up as the last person standing between Morngate and something that arrived from the east.*

---

## Structure

Linear: follows Solan from his first action in the Long Wars to the night Morngate falls. Three acts: the war years (Long Wars), the quiet years in Morngate's garrison, and the Post-Collapse arrival.

---

## Notes

*The ending is not a victory. Morngate falls. Solan survives. The story is about what that means — not resolution but continuation.*
