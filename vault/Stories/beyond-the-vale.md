# Beyond the Vale

*She had been waiting. The story is about what she saw when the waiting ended.*

---

## Structure

Linear but fragmentary: follows Lirien's walk from the Vale through the Known Lands to the northern coast. Each chapter is a different encounter along the road. The final chapter ends on a cliff above the sea. The next chapter does not exist.

---

## Notes

*The deliberate non-ending is the point. Her destination is a future story. This one is about saying goodbye to the Known Lands — which she does with great thoroughness and no visible regret.*
