## Notes & Connections

*Set in the early Post-Collapse, shortly after the Free Coast Declaration. The merchant roads physically survive — the routes, the inns, the bridges — but the safe-conduct agreements and the political entities that honored them are gone.*

*Thematically the inverse of Merchant Roads: that story was about using trade routes to build something. This story is about using them to hold something together after it has already broken.*
