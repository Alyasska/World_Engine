# The Wandering Grey

*A wanderer who has seen every age of the Known Lands finally intervenes in the last one.*

---

## Structure

Non-linear: opens at the Road of the Wanderer (Post-Collapse) and works backwards through his appearances at the First Council of Kings, the Siege of Stonecrown, and finally his arrival at the Vale of Whispers in the Age of Founding.

The backwards structure means the reader understands his final act before they understand where he came from — which mirrors how the Known Lands have always experienced him.

---

## Notes

*The central question of this story — what is Eredan? — is never answered. The story earns the non-answer.*
