# Last Sentinel

*Three hundred soldiers, sixty days, and a stranger who knew how it would end.*

---

## Structure

Tight and claustrophobic: told from inside Stonecrown's walls over the sixty-day siege. Durn's perspective throughout. Eredan appears in a single sustained thread from day forty-three to the end.

---

## Notes

*The three pieces of advice Eredan offers — none of which Durn takes, all of which are correct — are the structural spine of the story. The reader knows they're correct before Durn does.*
