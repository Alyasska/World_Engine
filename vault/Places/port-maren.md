# Port Maren

*The southern gate — where the Free Coast makes its stand against the sea and everything else.*

---

## Description

Port Maren faces the open sea and has the walls to prove it. Three languages are heard in the dockside markets on any given morning. The Free Coast's loose council meets here when it meets at all.

---

## Notes

*Mira Solhand's trade network has its southern anchor here. She is known by several names in different quarters of the port.*
