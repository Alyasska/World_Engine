# Solmark

*The city where the Compact was born and where most of its deals are still made.*

---

## Description

Solmark sits on the eastern shore of the Veiled Sea, facing the trade routes from the south. Ships from three coasts dock here. The dockside smells of salt, spice, and money. The Compact's council chambers are in a building that was originally a counting house.

---

## Notes

*Mira Solhand grew up here as a minor merchant family's third daughter. The city is her home ground and her network's center.*
