# Morngate

*The city that has always been worth taking — and never worth keeping.*

---

## Description

Morngate sits on a long eastern ridge, its pale stone walls catching the morning light before the rest of the world wakes. From the battlements on a clear day you can see all the way to the Eastern Hold and, on the best days, a silver line where the plains end and the unknown east begins.

The city was built to control the eastern trade routes and has done so, through war and peace and everything between, for as long as anyone has kept records.

---

## Notes

*The fall of Morngate in the Post-Collapse era is the defining event of that period. The Pale King's arrival ended four centuries of unbroken civic rule.*
