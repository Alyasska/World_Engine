# Vale of Whispers

*Whatever was said in this valley before history began, the stones have been repeating it ever since.*

---

## Description

The Vale sits in a fold of the northern highlands that is genuinely difficult to find. Travelers who know it is there still sometimes miss it. The standing stones are not quite in rows — more like a conversation that stopped mid-sentence.

Lirien has been here for as long as anyone's grandparents can remember. She gives no account of her origins and asks none from visitors.

---

## Notes

*The Vale's location at the far northern edge of the known world puts it outside any faction's territory. It has never been claimed, garrisoned, or taxed. It is one of very few places in the Known Lands that belongs to no one.*
