# The Grey Keep

*A fortress older than the records that describe it — used as prison, outpost, and now as something no one discusses.*

---

## Description

Perched on the northwestern cliffs of the Northern Reach, the Grey Keep is built from stone of a pale grey that turns nearly white in heavy fog. No founding record exists. House Voss inherited it when they inherited the north.

---

## Notes

*The Keep's garrison vanished in Year 38 of the Long Wars with no explanation. The event was covered up by Aran Voss. What actually happened is an open story question.*
