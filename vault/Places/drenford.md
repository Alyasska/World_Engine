# Drenford

*The town that exists because the river is crossable here, and trade exists because people must cross.*

---

## Description

Where the West River narrows, Drenford grew up on both banks. The bridge is older than the town's name. The market under it operates three days a week and informally every other day.

---

## Notes

*The Wanderer is known to frequent Drenford's bridge market. What he trades — if anything — is unclear.*
