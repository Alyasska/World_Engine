# Stonecrown

*It was not built to be comfortable. It was built to be impossible to take.*

---

## Description

The fortress grows out of the rock rather than sitting on it — the lower levels were cut directly into the promontory in an age before reliable stone-cutting tools, which makes the construction something of a mystery. The upper towers are younger, added over centuries, and they lack the precision of the foundation.

Durn Stoneshield has commanded here for eleven years. He knows every crack in the walls and has an opinion about every one of them.

---

## Notes

*The Siege of Stonecrown is the moment that defines this place. Before it, Stonecrown was a garrison posting — respected but unremarkable. After it, it became a symbol.*
