# Deepwater Docks

*Port Maren gets the glory. Deepwater Docks gets the heavy work.*

---

## Description

The bay here is naturally deep — ships that draw too much water for Port Maren's sandbars can dock here without difficulty. The town grew up around that single practical fact and has never needed another reason to exist.

Kessa of Saltmere passes through regularly on her trading routes. She considers it the most honest settlement on the coast: it wants your money and your labor, and does not pretend otherwise.

---

## Notes

*In the Post-Collapse period, Deepwater Docks becomes more important as Port Maren's authority fragments. The practical town outlasts the political ones.*
