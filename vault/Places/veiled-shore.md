# Veiled Shore

*A village where the water does not move and the birds do not land.*

---

## Description

On the southern shore of the Veiled Sea, Veiled Shore is a village that exists because someone has to harvest the reed that grows here. The sea beside it is unnaturally still — no waves, no ripple, no birds circling. Locals have learned not to comment on this.

## Notes

*The Wanderer has been seen here. The village's relationship to the Veiled Sea's strange properties is an open story question.*
