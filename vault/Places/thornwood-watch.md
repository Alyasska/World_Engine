# Thornwood Watch

*A single tower at the edge of a forest that no one has mapped.*

---

## Description

The Thornwood Watch stands at the boundary of the ancient Thornwood forest. The tower itself is unremarkable — four stories of stone, a fire beacon on top. What makes it notable is what it watches: the Thornwood is older than any political entity in the Known Lands and has never been fully explored.
