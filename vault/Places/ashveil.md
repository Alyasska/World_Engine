# Ashveil

*A city that was larger than Vareth and is now only ash.*

---

## Description

Two centuries of rain have not cleaned Ashveil's stones. They remain black. The rubble is extensive — evidence of a city that once rivaled or exceeded Vareth in size. There is no battle record, no siege account, no named army that claimed credit for its destruction.

Locals in nearby villages do not camp near Ashveil. They say the ash shifts.

---

## Notes

*The Burning of Ashveil is the central mystery of the Long Wars era. Its cause is intentionally unresolved. The Wanderer was seen leaving the site.*
