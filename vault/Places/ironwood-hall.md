# Ironwood Hall

*Every faction respects this place. Not one of them can explain why.*

---

## Description

The hall is impossible to date. The ironwood trees that grow through it take centuries to reach full size, and the trees here are enormous — which means the building was either constructed around existing ancient trees or the trees were planted when the hall was built, and no one knows which. Either possibility raises more questions than it answers.

Three separate founding documents of the Known Lands reference Ironwood Hall as the site of their first major agreements. None of the three describe a building — they describe a grove.

---

## Notes

*The Ambush at Ironwood is the event that breaks the tradition of neutrality. It is not an accident — the ambush is a deliberate statement that the old rules no longer apply.*
