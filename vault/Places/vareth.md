# Vareth

*The largest fortified city in the Northern Reach — where iron meets river.*

---

## Description

Built where the Var River narrows between the Ashspine foothills, Vareth is a city of coal smoke and cold water. The walls are old black stone, the iron gates older still. Six generations of House Voss have governed here without the gates ever being broken.

The city smells of forge-work and river mist. The north quarter is military; the south quarter is trade. The dividing line is a street the locals call the Long Step.

---

## History

Founded in Year 44 of the Age of Founding as a watchtower controlling the northern mountain passage. Within two generations it grew to a city proper.

During the Long Wars, Vareth was besieged for forty days (Year 12). Aran Voss broke the siege not through strategy but through a late-season flood that cut the Compact army's supply lines. The event cemented his rule.

---

## Culture & People

Vareth is not a warm city. The climate is harsh, the politics harder. House Voss rules by reputation and military strength — but the merchant quarter runs on its own logic. Travelers from the south find it cold in every sense.

---

## Notable Locations

- **The Iron Gates** — the city's defining feature; never breached in recorded history
- **Voss Keep** — seat of House Voss, on the northern hill above the river bend
- **The River Docks** — southern trade quarter; the only part of the city that laughs
