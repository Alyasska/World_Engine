# Saltmere

*It has no walls, no garrison, and no ambitions. That is why it is still standing.*

---

## Description

Saltmere is easy to miss on any map that doesn't specifically note it. The salt pans extend south of the main settlement — flat, white, geometric — and are visible from the sea on clear days. The village behind them is modest: a harbor with six boats, a salt warehouse, a tavern, and families who have been doing the same work for generations.

Kessa grew up here, learned the coast routes, and left. She sends salt home regularly. She sends money less often.
