# Eastern Hold

*Past this point, no map agrees with any other.*

---

## Description

The Hold was built at the eastern edge of surveyed territory — the point where caravan routes become footpaths and footpaths become guesswork. Its walls are functional rather than impressive, built for endurance against wind and long winters rather than against siege.

The Pale King arrived here first. The garrison did not report unusual activity for three months. By the time the first messenger reached Morngate, the Hold was already unrecognizable.

---

## Notes

*The Eastern Hold's transformation under the Pale King is the hinge moment of the Post-Collapse arc. Before: a dull but functional frontier station. After: the seat of something no one has a name for yet.*
