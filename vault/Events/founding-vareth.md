# Founding of Vareth

*Year 44, Age of Founding — a watchtower becomes a city.*

---

## What Happened

The first Voss planted a watchtower at the Var River narrows to control the only viable northern passage through the Ashspine foothills. Within two generations it had grown into Vareth.

---

## Notes

*The founding predates any records of who exactly built the first structure. House Voss claims it was always theirs.*
