# The Treaty of Solmark

*Year 31, The Long Wars — a ceasefire no one believes in, arranged by someone who was never supposed to be there.*

---

## What Happened

The Treaty of Solmark was not negotiated by ambassadors. It was arranged by Mira Solhand through her trading networks, using ledger entries and private dinners. Both the Northern Reach and the Compact Council signed the document without their military commanders present.

Both sides began planning to break it before the ink dried.

---

## Notes

*The treaty is still technically in effect. Whether it will survive the events of the main narrative is unresolved.*
