# The Burning of Ashveil

*Year 18, The Long Wars — the largest city on the southern coast is gone by dawn.*

---

## What Happened

Ashveil was a major city — by some accounts larger than Vareth. On the night of Year 18, it burned. By morning it was ash and rubble. No army has ever claimed responsibility. No siege accounts exist from either side of the Long Wars. No cause has been established.

The Wanderer was reported walking away from the direction of the smoke by three separate witnesses. He has never been asked about it on record.

---

## Notes

*The cause is permanently unresolved in the canon. Any story that claims to explain The Burning contradicts the mystery — do not write one unless it is deliberately an unreliable narrator.*
