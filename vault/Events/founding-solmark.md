# Charter of the Compact

*Year 91, Age of Founding — five houses sign a document that will outlast most of them.*

---

## What Happened

Five Solmark trading houses, all mid-tier competitors who could not individually resist larger political entities, signed a mutual defense and trade charter. The document created equal voting rights, a shared treasury for defense, and a prohibition on unilateral military action.

---

## Notes

*The charter's prohibition on unilateral military action was meant to prevent war. It has mostly worked, but created a council structurally incapable of responding quickly to anything.*
