# Thornwood Watch Abandoned

*Year 17, Post-Collapse — not a battle, not a disaster. Just the fires going out.*

---

## What Happened

Thornwood Watch was a small post. Four to six soldiers at any given time, tasked with observing the northern road through the forest. During the Long Wars it had strategic importance. After the Collapse, it mostly watched an empty road.

The garrison waited longer than they were required to. When no supplies and no orders came, the senior scout made the call. They left the fires banked, the doors locked, and walked south.

---

## Notes

*Nobody formally decommissioned Thornwood Watch. It simply ceased. The structure is intact; local travelers sometimes shelter in it.*
