# The Free Coast Declaration

*Year 9, Post-Collapse — the coast stops paying. Nobody comes to argue.*

---

## What Happened

For several years after the Long Wars, tax shipments inland had already been delayed, reduced, and disputed. Port Maren's council had been operating independently in all but name. In Year 9, the harbor master formalized what had already been true: no shipments, no tribute, no recognition of inland authority.

Drenford sent its own shorter declaration the following month. The Veiled Shore said nothing, which everyone interpreted the same way.

---

## Notes

*This event is intentionally undramatic. The Free Coast's independence was not won — it was simply noticed.*
