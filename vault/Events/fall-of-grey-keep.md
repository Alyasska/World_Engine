# The Fall of the Grey Keep

*Year 38, The Long Wars — the garrison is gone. The doors were locked from the inside.*

---

## What Happened

In Year 38 of the Long Wars, the Grey Keep's garrison stopped responding to regular communications. A relief party arrived to find the keep empty. No bodies. No blood. No evidence of struggle. The doors had been locked from the inside.

Aran Voss ordered the keep quietly reoccupied and issued no explanation to the garrison's families.
