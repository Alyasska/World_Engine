# Collapse of the Compact

*Year 3, Post-Collapse — the council that held the east together dissolves into a dispute over ink and debt.*

---

## What Happened

The Compact was built on five trading houses and a ledger. The Long Wars strained both. By Year 3 of the Post-Collapse, three of the founding houses had withdrawn from the council entirely. The Compact's central authority in Solmark became a rump body — technically extant, practically inert.

Vareth's last formal communiqué to the Compact council was never answered. After that, both sides treated the other as simply absent.

---

## Notes

*The Compact does not officially dissolve — it simply stops mattering. Solmark remains a city, just no longer the seat of anything.*
