# Kessa of Saltmere

*She has been to every port on this coast. She has been to some ports that aren't on any map. She finds maps aspirational rather than authoritative.*

---

## Background

Kessa grew up on her family's salt routes and took them over when her father's hands gave out. She expanded the routes south, then east, then into the Compact territories when the Long Wars created demand for goods that official supply lines couldn't provide.

She met Solan Vorne at Amber Fields when his village needed supplies and she happened to be passing through with a surplus. They have been occasional collaborators since, which she considers a more useful relationship than most.

---

## Story Role

Kessa provides the economic ground-level view of the Post-Collapse. She is the character who knows that the Pale King's emergence is dangerous not because of what it means symbolically, but because of what it does to the eastern trade routes.

---

## Notes

*She was present at the emergence of the Pale King because she had a delivery scheduled at Eastern Hold. She was the first civilian to see what had happened there and survive to report it. She has not been back.*
