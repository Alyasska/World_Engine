# Eredan the Grey

*He has been old for longer than the oldest records. At some point, that stops being a description and starts being a fact about time itself.*

---

## Background

Eredan appears in the founding records of the First Council of Kings — described, unremarkably, as a guest who offered three observations during the opening session and was not seen again for decades. The same figure, described identically, appears in Long Wars field dispatches, treaty records, and three separate private diaries from different centuries.

He does not explain this. If asked directly, he says the question is interesting and asks what the questioner is having for dinner.

---

## Story Role

Eredan is the witness character — he has seen everything and done very little, until the Post-Collapse arc when his intervention at the Road of the Wanderer changes the outcome of an otherwise settled question.

---

## Notes

*The model here is: wisdom without power is still wisdom. He knows how things end. He does not often say.*
