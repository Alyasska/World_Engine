# Lord Aran Voss

*A lord who built his reputation on strength, now unsure if strength can solve what is coming.*

---

## Background

Aran Voss took rule of Vareth at twenty-three, on the third day of a forty-day siege, with his father's body still in the hall. He broke the siege not through strategy but through luck — a flood cut the enemy supply lines.

He has governed for eleven years since. His public face is cold and certain. His private correspondence, known to few, reveals a man who reads more philosophy than military history.

---

## Story Role

Aran is the pivot of the Northern Reach narrative — the ruler who must decide whether the strength that has always defined his city is still enough for what is coming. He appears in *The First Winter* as a governor in a crisis that cannot be solved by command.

---

## Notes

*His relationship to the Grey Keep incident (event-fall-of-grey-keep) is unresolved. He ordered the keep quietly reoccupied and the incident not discussed. What he actually knows is an open story question.*
