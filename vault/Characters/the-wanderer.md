# The Wanderer

*Seen at Ashveil before the record-keepers arrived. Seen in Drenford sixty years later, looking the same age.*

---

## Background

Unknown. Documents spanning at least sixty years describe an elderly figure matching the same description, in the same places, with no apparent aging. He is not listed in any census, never pays taxes, never appears in any formal record except as a witness or bystander.

---

## Story Role

The Wanderer appears in *The Silence After* as the first outsider to reach Ashveil after The Burning. What he found — and chose not to report — is the central mystery of that story.

---

## Notes

*The Wanderer's nature is intentionally unresolved. He may be immortal, may be multiple people, may be a legend. Do not resolve this in vault notes.*
