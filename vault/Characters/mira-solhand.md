# Mira Solhand

*A merchant who understands the war better than any general, because she moves through both sides.*

---

## Background

Third daughter of a minor Solmark trading house. Her family's name opened doors; her own reputation kept them open after she went independent. She has cultivated relationships on both sides of the Long Wars — not through ideology but through usefulness.

---

## Story Role

Mira is the connective tissue of the Long Wars narrative. She appears in *Merchant Roads* as the person who actually arranges the Treaty of Solmark, working through ledgers and private meetings rather than official channels.

---

## Notes

*Her "surprisingly dangerous" trait is deliberately vague. She is not a soldier. The danger is something else.*
