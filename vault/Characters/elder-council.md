# The Compact Council

*Five houses, five votes, zero consensus — the government that accidentally survived the Long Wars.*

---

## Background

Founded by the Charter of the Compact (Year 91, Age of Founding). Five trading houses with equal votes and a mutual veto on military action. This structure was designed to prevent any one house from dragging the others into a costly war. It has worked, but not in the way the founders intended.

---

## Story Role

The Council appears in *Merchant Roads* as the institution Mira Solhand has to navigate around to arrange the Treaty of Solmark. Officially, they signed it. Unofficially, they were the last to know what they were signing.

---

## Notes

*Treated as a single character entity for the data model, though narratively it represents five distinct individuals. The individual council members are not yet named — that is Phase 3+ territory.*
