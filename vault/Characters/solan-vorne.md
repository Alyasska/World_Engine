# Solan Vorne

*He was not trained to be a hero. He was trained to know when the grain was ready to harvest. It turns out these skills overlap more than expected.*

---

## Background

Solan grew up in Amber Fields, which is exactly what it sounds like: a farming village, competent and quiet, producing grain for the markets to the north. He left at eighteen with a letter from the village elder that said, essentially, that he was reliable and observant and might be useful to someone.

He arrived in Morngate as a courier's assistant. He stayed because the war needed people who were reliable and observant.

---

## Story Role

Solan is the ground-level view of the Long Wars and the Post-Collapse. He does not command armies or make treaties — he moves through the war at the level where wars are actually experienced, and reports what he sees.

---

## Notes

*The charge he leads at Amber Fields is not a glorious military maneuver. It is a defensive action to save his village during a Long Wars skirmish. It works. That is enough.*
