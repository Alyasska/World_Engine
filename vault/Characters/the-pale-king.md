# The Pale King

*No one who has seen him closely agrees on what they saw. They agree on the feeling: that the future has already happened and he is returning from it.*

---

## Background

He arrived at Eastern Hold in the first year of the Post-Collapse — which some scholars argue was not a coincidence but a cause. The garrison's commander sent three routine reports during that period. The fourth report was not from the commander.

What followed was not a conquest in the usual sense. Eastern Hold did not fall. It transformed. The garrison remained at their posts. The walls remained intact. The only thing that changed was who the garrison was loyal to, and why.

---

## Story Role

The Pale King is the force that gives the Post-Collapse arc its shape. He does not appear in many scenes — he operates through consequence and absence. His presence is felt in what stops working, what stops arriving, what stops being reported.
