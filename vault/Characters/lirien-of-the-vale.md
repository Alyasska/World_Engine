# Lirien of the Vale

*She has been there longer than the standing stones. Possibly she was there when the stones arrived.*

---

## Background

No founding document mentions Lirien's arrival at the Vale. She is mentioned in passing in texts that are themselves ancient — always as someone who has already been there for a long time.

She and Eredan know each other. Neither has described the nature of their relationship to any third party. Their first recorded meeting, in the Arrival of Eredan, involves a lengthy conversation that witnesses reported as entirely in a language they did not recognize.

---

## Story Role

Lirien is the character who knows. The story arc of *Beyond the Vale* follows her departure from the Vale — the first time she has left in living memory — and the question of what she is leaving behind and where she is going.

---

## Notes

*Her departure at the end of the Post-Collapse arc is the most significant unresolved narrative thread in the world. It is deliberately unexplained. The question of where she went is a future story.*
